**Completion Date:** April 30, 2026  
**Objective:** Transform Ampicked from a generic WordPress hosting comparison site into THE primary teacher resource platform that educators return to for advancing their website journey.

### Before
- Generic WordPress hosting guidance

## Positioning Statements

### Primary Positioning
**"Ampicked is where teachers come to advance their website journey."**

The site helps educators at every stage:
- Launch their first classroom site
- Engage students through online interaction
- Scale to multiple projects and classrooms

- **Honest:** No affiliate bias, just practical guidance
