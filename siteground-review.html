What is SiteGround?

SiteGround is one of the world's leading web hosting providers, headquartered in Bulgaria with data centers across six continents. Founded in 2003, SiteGround has earned a reputation as the premium WordPress hosting solution, trusted by developers, agencies, and businesses worldwide. While priced higher than budget alternatives, SiteGround's superior performance, security, and support justify the investment for serious sites.
