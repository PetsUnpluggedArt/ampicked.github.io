## Bluehost Review

Bluehost is one of the most popular WordPress hosting providers, officially recommended by WordPress.org. It's a great choice for beginners and small business sites.

### Quick Facts

- **Best for:** Beginners, hobby sites, small business
- **Type:** Shared WordPress Hosting
- **Price:** $2.95-4.95/month
- **Uptime:** 99.9%
- **Support:** 24/7 Chat & Email

### What We Like

- **WordPress.org Official Recommendation** — They're officially recommended by WordPress.org, which means they meet WordPress standards.
- **Easy Setup** — One-click WordPress installation, beginner-friendly interface.
- **24/7 Support** — Available via chat and email, though response times can vary.
- **Affordable** — One of the cheapest WordPress hosting options.

### What We Don't Like

- **Limited Performance** — Shared servers mean your site shares resources with hundreds of others.
- **Slow Under Load** — Performance degrades when traffic spikes.
- **Overcrowded Servers** — The popularity of Bluehost means servers can be crowded.
- **Renewal Price Shock** — Intro rates of $2.95 jump to $8.95+ after the first year.

### Performance Metrics

- **Average Load Time:** 2.5-3.5 seconds (acceptable, not great)
- **Uptime:** 99.9% (good)
- **Server Response Time:** Varies by server load

### Who Should Choose Bluehost?

- First-time website owners
- Hobby blogs and portfolios
- Small business sites with minimal traffic
- Budget-conscious users who don't mind limited performance

### Who Should Look Elsewhere?

- Revenue-generating websites
- E-commerce stores
- High-traffic blogs
- Users who need premium performance

### Getting Started

1. Choose your plan (Basic, Plus, or Choice Plus)
2. Register a domain or use an existing one
3. Complete setup with their guided installer
4. Launch your WordPress site

### Final Verdict

Bluehost is a solid choice for WordPress beginners. The official WordPress.org recommendation is meaningful—they meet WordPress standards. However, if your site generates revenue or expects consistent traffic, you'll outgrow shared hosting quickly.

**Rating:** ⭐⭐⭐⭐ (4/5) for beginners and hobby sites
