What is Hostinger?

Hostinger is a Lithuania-based web hosting provider that has rapidly grown to become one of the world's most popular choices for WordPress hosting. With over 6 million users worldwide, Hostinger has proven that exceptional value doesn't require premium pricing. The company focuses on delivering strong performance, user-friendly tools, and responsive support at competitive rates, making it ideal for budget-conscious entrepreneurs and small businesses.
