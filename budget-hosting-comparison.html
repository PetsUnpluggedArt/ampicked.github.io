Budget Hosting Overview

Budget web hosting providers offer competitive pricing while maintaining adequate performance and support for small business websites and WordPress sites. Unlike premium hosts charging $10-20+ monthly, budget hosts deliver similar functionality at $2.69-$5.95 monthly. The key distinction is shared hosting infrastructure—resources are shared among many users, unlike dedicated servers. For sites with moderate traffic and typical performance requirements, budget hosting provides excellent value.
